Recipe for Honey Garlic Chicken in uder 15 Minutes

Honey Garlic Chicken is an epic 12 minute chicken breast recipe with a magic 5 ingredient Honey Garlic Sauce! This is a terrific back pocket quick dinner idea for busy nights that’s made with pantry staples. It’s quite amazing how so few ingredients can transform into such a tasty sauce for chicken!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Garlic
  • Vinegar
  • Honey
  • Soy Sauce
  • Butter
  • Chicken Breasts

Easy step by step method

  1. Split chicken breast in half so 2 breasts makes 4 pieces
  2. Dust chicken with flour as it gives the chicken a slight crust so the sauce clings to it better
  3. Sear chicken, add butter, garlic, vinegar, soy sauce, honey.
  4. Simmer until the sauce thickens. Turn chicken to coat in the amazing honey garlic sauce.
